Admissions

Dipak school offer a place in our school is based on merit and number of places available, and to gain admission applicant must meet the required standards. We ensure to make our admission process as simple as possible to ensure that all prospective parents enjoy a hassel free application experience.

Our practice of school admissions code ensures an objective admissions process that promotes equity and fair access for all.Admission for all entering students shall be based on the data submitted, as required by the admission cell. for more information Contact Us.
